Ocean Freight – Import And Export

Fast and secured delivery on scheduled time

Ark Line is fully Licensed as a Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC) and a bonded ocean freight forwarder. With over 20 years of experience in international ocean freight, we offer a complete range of ocean cargo shipping services. Ark Line has significant industry experience and the financial stability you can depend on.

We have built an extensive network of international ocean freight agents in almost every country in the world. Our experienced global partners enable Ark Line’s customers to import and export from virtually any city worldwide.

Ark Line can handle all of your International ocean freight needs. Our expert team of import and export specialists understands the importance of choosing a trusted and experienced ocean freight forwarding company.

A host of costly issues can arise when working with an inexperienced freight forwarder or consultant. Ark Line takes the worry out of the equation.

WE CAN DO:

  • Full containers (FCL)
  • Less than container load shipments (LCL)
  • General sea cargo shipping
  • Specialized items
  • Over dimensional loads
  • Heavy equipment
  • Hazardous materials (hazmat)
  • High-value cargo

OCEAN TO DOOR SERVICE

When a shipment arrives at a port, Ark Line can transport it to any domestic location using one of our dependable trucking carriers. Ark Line customers have the option of drop-and-pull, and drayage services for full container loads (FCL).

Ark Line also offers cost-lowering consolidation services by combining less than container load shipments (LCL) that are going to the same destination port.

Conversely, when exporting, Ark Line collaborates with our foreign partners to provide door-to-door international ocean freight shipping and customs brokerage services - guiding your shipments through the complex customs process into the destination country successfully.

OVERSIZED FREIGHT SERVICES FOR INTERNATIONAL SHIPMENTS

Ark Line is your oversized freight specialists. We offer professional and reliable international shipping services for oversized or project freight.

Discover what sets Ark Line apart by contacting us now to learn about our oversized freight shipping services including heavy equipment, heavy machinery, or project freight, and customs clearance. We also provide lift-on lift-off freight service.

Ark Line offers standard 20’ and 40’ ocean shipping containers and the following specialized equipment international import and export:

  • 40’ and 45' High Cube
  • 20’ & 40’ Open Tops
  • 20’ & 40’ Flat Racks
  • 20’ & 40’ Reefers
  • 45’ Standard

Our global network of agents will be looking out to receive your goods at the point of delivery (be that commercial or personal address, or warehouse or port), organize any exchange of documents and, if necessary, send the cargo on to the next stage of its journey.

We can provide warehousing if you need it, using one of our warehousing partners. We’ll be watching with our global tracking system every step of the way and we’ll let you know as soon as your goods reach their final destination.
